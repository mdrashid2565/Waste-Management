What We Do & Why We Do

At We Open Society Foundation, we help people in need by using extra stuff. We also stop waste. Why do we do this? Let's find out.

In our world today, some people have a lot, and others don't have enough. We want to share what's extra, like food and clothes, with those who need it. This way, we make the world better. We care about everyone having what they need, and we don't like wasting things. We're here to help people and the planet, making life fairer and more sustainable.
